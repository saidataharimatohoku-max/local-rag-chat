# Sample Employee Handbook

## Working Hours

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
Employees may use flexible hours between 7:00 AM and 7:00 PM as long as they
complete eight hours per day and are available for core hours from 10:00 AM
to 3:00 PM.

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per year, plus 10 public
holidays. Unused vacation of up to 5 days may be carried over into the next
calendar year. Sick leave is granted at 10 days per year and does not carry
over.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
A reliable internet connection and a quiet workspace are required for remote
work days.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days with a valid receipt.
Approved expenses are reimbursed in the next payroll cycle. Travel booked
through the company portal is billed directly and does not require
reimbursement.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to act with integrity, treat colleagues with
respect, and protect confidential company information. Violations should be
reported to the Human Resources department.
